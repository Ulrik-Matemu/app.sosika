Privacy Policy for Sosika

Effective Date: January 11, 2026

Welcome to Sosika. We are an online food delivery aggregator operating in Tanzania. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application and services.

Note to Users: Sosika acts as an intermediary (aggregator) between customers and independent food vendors. We do not prepare food; we facilitate the discovery and delivery process.

1. Information We Collect

  • Location Data: We collect location information through user entry (searching addresses or selecting on a map) to identify nearby restaurants and facilitate accurate delivery.
  • User Inputs ("Mood"): We collect information regarding your current preference or "mood" to provide personalized food recommendations.
  • Contact Information: Name, phone number, and delivery address provided during the checkout process.
  • Transaction Details: Information about the orders you place via our platform.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:

  • Provide food recommendations based on your location and mood.
  • Share your delivery details with our delivery partners and the selected restaurant.
  • Send order status updates and customer support communications.
  • Comply with Tanzanian legal and regulatory requirements.

3. Data Sharing & Third Parties

As an aggregator, we share necessary information with:

  • Restaurants/Vendors: To prepare your specific order.
  • Delivery Riders: Your address and contact number for fulfillment.
  • Payment Processors: We use secure third-party payment gateways. We do not store your full credit card or mobile money PINs on our servers.

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. In accordance with the Tanzania Personal Data Protection Act, we ensure that your personal information is stored securely and only for as long as necessary to provide our services.

5. Your Rights & Account Deletion

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data.

To delete your account: You may request account deletion through the "Settings" menu in the app or by emailing us at support@sosika.co.tz. Upon request, we will delete your personal data, excluding information we are legally required to retain for tax or audit purposes.
